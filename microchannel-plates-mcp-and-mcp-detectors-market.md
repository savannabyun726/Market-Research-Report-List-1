<p><p>Microchannel Plates (MCP) are highly effective electron multipliers that consist of a dense array of tiny channels, facilitating the amplification of incoming electron events. When electrons enter a channel, they collide with the walls, causing secondary electrons to be emitted, and this process repeats, ultimately generating a measurable signal. MCP detectors utilize these plates to convert incoming photons or charged particles into an electrical signal, commonly used in applications such as spectroscopy, imaging, and detection in various scientific and industrial fields.</p><p>The Microchannel Plates (MCP) and MCP Detectors Market is poised for significant growth, expected to expand at a CAGR of 12.7% during the forecast period.
