<p><strong>The Phenolic Resin For Electronic Products Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermoplastic Phenolic Resin</li><li>Thermosetting Phenolic Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Phenolic resin in the electronic products market is categorized into two types: thermoplastic and thermosetting phenolic resins. Thermoplastic phenolic resin is characterized by its ability to be re-melted and reshaped, offering flexibility and ease of processing, making it suitable for applications requiring high impact resistance. On the other hand, thermosetting phenolic resin undergoes a curing process that results in a rigid, durable structure, ideal for high-temperature applications and providing excellent electrical insulation, which is essential for electronic components.</p></p>
