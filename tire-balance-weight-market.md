<p><p>The Tire Balance Weight market includes two main types: Clip-On and Adhesive. Clip-On weights are metal pieces attached to the wheel rim using clips, commonly used for steel rims due to their durability and ease of installation. Adhesive weights are stuck to the inner surface of alloy or custom rims using strong adhesive, offering a cleaner look and preventing rim damage. Both types ensure balanced tire rotation, enhancing vehicle performance, safety, and tire longevity.</p></p>
