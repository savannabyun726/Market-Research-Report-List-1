<p><p>The rigid PCB substrate resin market comprises several key types, including epoxy resin, BT (bismaleimide-triazine) resin, ABF (Aromatic Bismaleimide Formulation) resin, and other specialized resins. Epoxy resin is widely used for its excellent adhesion and thermal stability. BT resin offers enhanced mechanical properties and lower moisture absorption, making it suitable for high-performance applications. ABF resin excels in high-frequency applications due to its low dielectric constant. Other resins include polyimide and polyester, catering to specific performance needs in various electronic devices.</p></p>
