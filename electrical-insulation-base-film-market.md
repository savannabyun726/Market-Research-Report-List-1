<p><p>The electrical insulation base film market includes several types, each serving different applications. Polyimide films offer high thermal stability and excellent dielectric properties, making them ideal for aerospace and electronics. Polyester films are known for their moisture resistance and mechanical strength, commonly used in capacitors and transformers. Fluoropolymer films provide exceptional chemical resistance and low friction, suitable for demanding environments. Vinyl films are cost-effective and versatile, often used in a variety of household and industrial electrical insulation applications.</p></p>
