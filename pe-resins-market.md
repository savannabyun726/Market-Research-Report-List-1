<p><p>The polyethylene (PE) resins market comprises three primary types: High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE), Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE), and Linear Low-Density Polyethylene (LLDPE). HDPE is known for its strength and rigidity, making it ideal for containers and industrial applications. LDPE offers flexibility and is commonly used in packaging films and bags. LLDPE combines the properties of HDPE and LDPE, providing enhanced stretchability and puncture resistance, often utilized in flexible packaging and agricultural films. Each type serves distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
