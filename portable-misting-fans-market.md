<p><p>The portable misting fans market is categorized into three types based on pressure levels: high, mid, and low pressure. High-pressure misting fans operate at elevated pressure, delivering a fine mist for efficient cooling, ideal for large outdoor spaces. Mid-pressure fans provide moderate misting suitable for smaller areas, balancing performance and cost. Low-pressure misting fans are typically more affordable and easier to install, generating a coarser mist, making them suitable for residential use and light commercial applications.</p></p>
