<p><strong>The Polypropylene Film Capacitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plain Polypropylene Film Capacitors</li><li>Metallised Polypropylene Film Capacitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polypropylene film capacitor market features two main types: plain polypropylene film capacitors and metallised polypropylene film capacitors. Plain polypropylene film capacitors utilize thin polypropylene films for electrical insulation and are known for their stability and reliability in low-frequency applications. In contrast, metallised polypropylene film capacitors have a metal layer deposited on the film, enhancing energy density and self-healing properties. This makes them suitable for various applications, including power electronics and audio equipment, where efficiency and performance are crucial.</p></p>
