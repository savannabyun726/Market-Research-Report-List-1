<p><p>Air Traffic Control (ATC) is a service provided by ground-based controllers who direct aircraft on the ground and in the air to ensure safe, orderly, and efficient air traffic flow. ATC systems manage takeoffs, landings, and en-route navigation, utilizing communication, navigation, and surveillance technologies to prevent collisions and minimize delays.</p><p>The Air Traffic Control (ATC) Market is experiencing steady growth, driven by increasing air traffic, modernization of aging infrastructure, and the adoption of advanced technologies. The market is expected to grow at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 4.7% during the forecast period. This growth is fueled by the rising demand for air travel, particularly in emerging economies, and the need for enhanced safety and efficiency in airspace management.</p><p>Key trends in the ATC market include the integration of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning for predictive analytics, the deployment of satellite-based navigation systems like ADS-B (Automatic Dependent Surveillance-Broadcast), and the shift toward digital and automated ATC solutions. Additionally, the development of unmanned traffic management systems for drones and urban air mobility (UAM) is creating new opportunities in the sector. Governments and aviation authorities worldwide are investing in modernizing ATC infrastructure to accommodate future air traffic demands and improve operational resilience.</p></p>
