<p><p>Advanced High-Strength Steels (AHSS) are categorized into three distinct market types based on their alloy content: Low Alloy, Medium Alloy, and High Alloy. Low Alloy steels contain a minimal percentage of alloying elements, offering enhanced strength and formability. Medium Alloy steels provide a balance of strength and ductility, suitable for structural applications. High Alloy steels, with a higher concentration of alloying elements, deliver superior performance in demanding environments, making them ideal for automotive and high-stress applications.</p></p>
