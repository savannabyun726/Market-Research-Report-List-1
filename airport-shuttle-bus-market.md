<p><p>An airport shuttle bus is a dedicated transportation service designed to ferry passengers between airports and nearby locations such as hotels, parking lots, or city centers. This growth is fueled by the rising preference for eco-friendly and sustainable transportation solutions, as many shuttle services are transitioning to electric or hybrid vehicles to reduce carbon emissions. Additionally, advancements in technology, such as real-time tracking and mobile booking platforms, are enhancing passenger convenience and operational efficiency.</p><p>Emerging trends in the market include the integration of smart technologies for route optimization, the adoption of autonomous shuttle buses, and partnerships between shuttle service providers and ride-hailing platforms.
