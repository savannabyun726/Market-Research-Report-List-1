<p><p>The Surface Active Agents (Surfactants) market is categorized into four main types based on their molecular charge. **Anionic Surfactants** have a negative charge, widely used in detergents and cleaning products due to their strong cleaning ability. **Cationic Surfactants** carry a positive charge, commonly found in fabric softeners and disinfectants for their antimicrobial properties. **Zwitterionic Surfactants** have both positive and negative charges, offering mildness and stability, ideal for personal care products. **Non-Ionic Surfactants** are neutral, used in emulsifiers and stabilizers, valued for their compatibility and low foaming properties.</p></p>
