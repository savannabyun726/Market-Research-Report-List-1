<p><p>Building spandrel glass is a type of architectural glass used primarily in mid- to high-rise buildings, serving both functional and aesthetic purposes. Typically installed between window sections or portions of curtain walls, it is often opaque or translucent, designed to hide structural elements like floor slabs while enhancing the building's façade. The use of spandrel glass provides insulation and energy efficiency, contributing to a building's overall performance and sustainability.</p><p>The Building Spandrel Glass Market is projected to experience significant growth, with an expected CAGR of 12.6% during the forecast period.
