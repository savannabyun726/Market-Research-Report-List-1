<p><ul><li>Tight Head Reconditioned Steel Drums</li><li>Open Head Reconditioned Steel Drums</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The reconditioned steel drums market is primarily divided into two types: tight head and open head reconditioned steel drums. Tight head reconditioned steel drums have a sealed top with fittings for easy pouring and are typically used for liquids, offering better protection against spills. Open head reconditioned steel drums feature a removable lid, allowing for easy loading and unloading of solids and powders. Both types provide sustainable packaging solutions while maintaining safety and durability for various industrial applications.</p></p>
