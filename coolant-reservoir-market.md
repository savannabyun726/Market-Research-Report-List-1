<p><p>A coolant reservoir, also known as a coolant overflow tank or expansion tank, is a component in a vehicle's cooling system that stores excess coolant. The reservoir ensures efficient heat dissipation and prolongs the engine's lifespan by maintaining optimal operating temperatures.</p><p>The Coolant Reservoir Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing vehicle production, advancements in automotive technologies, and the rising demand for efficient cooling systems.
