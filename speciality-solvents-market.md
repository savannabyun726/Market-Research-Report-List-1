<p><p>The **Specialty Solvents Market** includes **Aromatic Hydrocarbon Solvents**, **Alcohol Solvents**, and **Amine Solvents**, each serving distinct industrial applications. Aromatic hydrocarbon solvents, like toluene and xylene, are widely used in paints, coatings, and adhesives due to their strong solvency. Alcohol solvents, such as ethanol and isopropanol, are versatile, used in pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and cleaning products for their evaporation rates and solubility. Amine solvents, like ethanolamine, are essential in gas treatment, agrochemicals, and personal care for their reactivity and pH regulation properties.</p></p>
