<p><p>The Gallium Nitride (GaN) epiwafers market features several substrates, each offering unique advantages. GaN-on-Silicon Carbide (SiC) provides superior thermal management and efficiency, ideal for high-power devices. GaN-on-GaN allows for better lattice matching and improved electron mobility, enhancing performance. Other substrates may include various composite materials tailored for specific applications.</p></p>
