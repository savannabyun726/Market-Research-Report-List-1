<p><p>Composite diamond coatings are advanced materials applied to various surfaces to enhance durability, wear resistance, and performance. The market primarily comprises two types: Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) and Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD). CVD involves chemical reactions that deposit diamond material onto a substrate, resulting in high-quality, uniform coatings ideal for precision tools. In contrast, PVD utilizes physical processes to vaporize and condense materials onto surfaces, offering flexibility in coating properties and applications across industries such as aerospace and electronics.</p></p>
