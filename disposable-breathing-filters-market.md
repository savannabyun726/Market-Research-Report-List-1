<p><ul><li>Mechanical Filter</li><li>Electrostatic Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Disposable Breathing Filters market includes two main types: Mechanical Filters and Electrostatic Filters. Mechanical Filters physically trap particles through a barrier, such as a porous material, effectively reducing airborne contaminants. In contrast, Electrostatic Filters utilize electrostatic charges to attract and capture particles, enhancing filtration efficiency. Both types are designed for short-term use in various applications, including healthcare and industrial settings, ensuring cleaner air for users by minimizing exposure to harmful pollutants and allergens.</p></p>
