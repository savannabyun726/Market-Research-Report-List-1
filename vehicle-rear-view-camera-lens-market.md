<p><p>The Vehicle Rear View Camera Lens market primarily features two types: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) cameras. CCD cameras are known for their high image quality, especially in low-light conditions, making them ideal for premium vehicles. CMOS cameras, on the other hand, are more cost-effective, energy-efficient, and compact, catering to mid-range and budget vehicles. Both technologies enhance safety by providing clear rear-view visuals, with CCD excelling in performance and CMOS offering affordability and versatility for broader market adoption.</p></p>
