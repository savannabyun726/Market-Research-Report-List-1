<p><ul><li>Line Scan Camera</li><li>Area Scan Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The machine vision camera market primarily consists of two types: line scan cameras and area scan cameras. Line scan cameras capture images one line at a time, making them ideal for continuous operations, such as web inspections in industrial settings. In contrast, area scan cameras capture an entire image at once, suited for applications requiring detailed images of static scenes, like quality control in manufacturing. Both types play crucial roles in automation, inspection, and analysis across various industries.</p></p>
