<p><strong>The EV Battery Thermal Management System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Management System</li><li>Passive Management System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The EV Battery Thermal Management System market comprises two main types: Active Management Systems and Passive Management Systems. Active Management Systems use external energy sources, like pumps and fans, to regulate battery temperature, ensuring optimal performance and longevity. In contrast, Passive Management Systems rely on heat sinks or phase change materials to naturally dissipate heat without requiring additional energy input. Both systems aim to enhance safety, efficiency, and reliability of electric vehicle batteries, catering to varying technological preferences and cost considerations.</p></p>
